How to become 10 times developer

  • Focuse on the task at hand, improve the ability to focus by minimizing all the possible distractions. I.e. Close all other programs, put phone on silent mode to allow yourself to be distraction free.

  • One can improve the ability the focus by training. People are not born with it, it is a skill that can and should be trained.

  • Pomodoro was mentioned by name. Focusing techniques give you tools and the ability to work in a focused state. Experiment with them and try to find the best baseline for yourself.

  • Slicing the task in smaller entities can give you more achievable goals and you can realize the big picture more easily. This is one of the components that make it easier to focus.

  • Realize and comprehend that focused work is more productive. There is a difference in working 2 hours in a focused state than working 8 hours while constantly being distracted.

  • You have limited resources, use them accordingly. Try to figure out what is essential and concentrate on that.
